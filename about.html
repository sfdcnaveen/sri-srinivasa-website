About Us

Welcome to Sri Srinivasa Weights & Measures, proprietor Pasupuleti Srinivasulu. We have been proudly serving the Kavali community for more than 25 years, providing reliable sales and service for all types of electronic and manual weighing machines.

Our commitment is to quality, accuracy, and excellent customer service. We understand the importance of precise measurements in your business and daily life, and we strive to provide the best solutions tailored to your needs. Over the decades, we've built a reputation for reliability and expertise in the field of weights and measures.

Whether you need a simple scale for your shop or a complex industrial weighing system, Sri Srinivasa Weights & Measures is here to help.
